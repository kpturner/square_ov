# Officers

## What this feature does

This feature allows you to add, change or remove officers from your official visit, including the VIP. Below the list of officers, the system will show you the procession order for the officers you have entered. It will also show you the list of ranks in order for a visual reference. You can provide the following information for an officer:

- A checkbox to indicate that the officer is expected to attend. Sometimes an allocated officer may not be able to attend, so you can use this to exclude them from everything whilst still keeping a record of the fact that they were allocated to the official visit.
- Name
- Provincial rank (if applicable)
- A "lightening bolt" button to provide a provincial rank override (for officers that hold an active office but also have a higher provincial rank). Those with an override will be indicated by a red dot next to the lightening bolt.
- A checkbox to indicate that the provincial rank is "active" as opposed to "past"
- The year the provincial rank was conferred (if applicable)
- Position in the procession. In almost every case you should leave this as "automatic" in order to benefit from the automatic ordering of officers in the procession. However, if you have a special case where you need to override the automatic ordering, you can select a specific position in the procession for that officer. For example, as the DC you would place yourself at "head_of_south". The VIP would be positioned as "VIP". The Sword Bearer would be position as "sword_bearer". The Standard Bearer would be positioned as "standard_bearer". All other officers should be left as "automatic" unless you have a special case. An important note: You can even place an officer in a specific row and column in the procession, but it is recommended that you only do that if you have a very good reason to do so. The automatic ordering of officers in the procession is designed to be correct in almost every case, and it is recommended that you use it unless you have a special case.
- A checkbox to indicate that the officer is a Grand Officer
- If the previous checkbox is ticked, you have a dropdown box to select the officer's Grand Rank.
- A checkbox to indicate that the grand rank is "active" as opposed to "past"
- The year that the grand rank was conferred (if applicable)
- A checkbox to exclude the officer from the procession. This is useful if you want to have a seat reservation for the officer but not include them in the procession. For example, if you have a Past Provincial Grand Master who is attending the official visit but is not taking part in an APGMs procession, you would tick this checkbox for that officer.

## How do I add an officer?

Click the "Add Officer" button to add a new officer to your official visit. You will have two options, either to select an officer from the master list of active officers for the year in question, or to create a new officer from scratch (when adding a provincial officer who is not active for ther year).

In the first instance you will be prompted for the masonic year that you are interested in. By default it will be the the current masonic year, which changes after each AGM. You can also select a previous year if you are interested in creating an official visit from a previous year.

Below that is a searchable dropdown box for you to find and select the officer that you want to add. Once you have selected the officer, click the "OK" button to add him.

Alternatively you can ignore that and simply click "OK" without selecting an officer. You will then be prompted to enter the officer's name and details in the main officer list.

IMPORTANT NOTE: Changes you make to officers directly in the officer list will only be remembered if you click the "Save Changes" button!

## What are the options I can take for each officer?

- Contact details - You can ensure that the officers phone number and email address are correct here. The email address is important if you wish to make use of the "Email the team" button at the top of the page. If you use this option and there is an officer without an email address, you will be prompted to correct the situation first.
- Delete - This will delete the officer from your official visit. You will be prompted to confirm this action before it is completed.

## What are the options I can take for my procession

- Seat reservations - You can click this button to see, and print, the seat reservations for you officers. You can optionally also print spares.
- Attendance report - You can click this button to see, and print, the expected attendance report for your visit.
- Email the team - Click this button to create an email in your device email client with all your officers BCCd. Every officer must have an email address defined for this to work.
- Help - Click this button at the top of the page for interactive help. If you are reading this it is a fair chance you have already found this option!
- Support - From the Help page you can click the "Support" button to send a request for support to the administrator.

Use this option after you have created your official visit to add/change/remove officers and VIPs.
